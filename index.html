FAQ

Will Studyr be open source?
If the funding goals are met, Studyr will be open sourced under the GPLv2 license. However, additional Studyr Packs released by the community may not fall under the same conditions.
What technologies are being used to build Studyr?
The initial release of Studyr will be a webapp available on all platforms (Windows/Mac/Linux/Android/iOS), and designed with mobiles first. In due time, these apps may be ported to native applications if there is sufficient demand.
